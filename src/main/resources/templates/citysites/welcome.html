CitySites

Know Where To Go

Description of App: CitySites is an app for those looking for more to do in our great city of San Antonio and soon, a city near you! Powered by Google and the great minds of the CitySites development team, you, the user, will be able to find fun activities, save your favorite places and even add your own activities. Have a public function you want the populace to know about, make an account and add it to the map! See what other users have going on and go make some new friends! There is a fantastic city of wonders out there just waiting for you to explore. CitySites will show you where to go!
